Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how Selecto collects, uses and protects your information while you connect with others using audio calls and chats.

Privacy

Welcome to Selecto.

Selecto is a communication platform that allows users to connect with other users using audio calls and chat facilities. Users can recharge their wallets, communicate with other users, earn money according to platform policies, and withdraw their earnings.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your information responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we share it, and how we protect your information while using Selecto.

By accessing or using Selecto, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Policy

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Mobile number
  • OTP verification status
  • Gender
  • State
  • Preferred language
  • Interests
  • Selected avatar from avatars provided within the app

We do not collect email addresses and do not allow users to upload custom profile pictures.

Communication Information

  • Chat messages
  • Voice call details
  • Call duration
  • Call timestamps
  • Recent call history
  • User interaction records

Wallet & Payment Information

  • Recharge amount
  • Transaction details
  • Wallet balance
  • Withdrawal amount
  • Withdrawal status
  • Aadhaar number
  • PAN number
  • Name as per Aadhaar
  • UPI ID

Device Information

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • IP address
  • Device identifiers
  • Crash reports
  • Network information

How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Verify mobile number using OTP
  • Enable chat and audio call services
  • Process wallet recharges
  • Verify withdrawals
  • Prevent fraud and misuse
  • Improve app performance
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with legal requirements

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

Information may be shared with payment partners, identity verification providers, cloud hosting providers, analytics services, and government authorities when required by law.

Chat & Call Privacy

Private chats and calls are intended only for communication between users. We do not publicly disclose chat content. Call and chat records may be retained for fraud prevention, dispute resolution, legal compliance, and platform security.

Data Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, alteration, or disclosure. However, no internet-based service can guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to provide services, maintain transaction records, process withdrawals, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations.

Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your account. Certain financial or legal records may be retained if required by applicable laws.

Children's Privacy

Selecto is intended only for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18 years of age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app or on our website along with the updated effective date.

Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact us through the support options available within the Selecto application.
